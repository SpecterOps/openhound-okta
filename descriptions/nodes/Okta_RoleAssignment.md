## Overview

To help visualize role assignments in BloodHound, Okta_RoleAssignment nodes are created for each role assignment in Okta. These nodes represent the relationship between a [user](Okta_User.md), [group](Okta_Group.md), or [application](Okta_Application.md) and a role ([built-in](Okta_Role.md) or [custom](Okta_CustomRole.md)).
